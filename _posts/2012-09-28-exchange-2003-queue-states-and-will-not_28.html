--- layout: post title: |- Exchange 2003 queue states "retry" and will not unfreeze to state "Ready" date: '2012-09-28T06:42:00.001-07:00' author: syswow64 tags: - enterprise-it - Exchange - queues - exchange 2003 - smart host - microsoft modified_time: '2017-08-03T00:42:55.476-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4452544758446808584.post-3284540516916412530 blogger_orig_url: https://www.syswow64.co.uk/2012/09/exchange-2003-queue-states-and-will-not_28.html --- My issue in particular related to the decommissioning of a Exchange 2003 server.  This server was turned off; then another Exchange server stopped sending/ receiving emails.  However, all other Exchange instances were working correctly.

Cause was tracked down to the "Smart host" settings entry referring to the decommissioned server.

Exchange server> Protocols> SMTP> Default SMTP Virtual Server > Right click Properties

Delivery Tab > Advanced > "Smart Host" entry

In my case i could simply remove the entry as it was not required.
