Principle Investigator

    Zexian Zeng received Ph.D. in Biomedical Informatics from Northwestern University in 2019. He has been a postdoc fellow with the Department of Data Science and Biostastics at the Dana-Farber Cancer Institute and Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health until 2022. Zexian joined Peking University in 2022 and is affliated with Center for Quantitative Biology (CQB) and Peking-Tsinghua Center for Life Science (CLS). Zexian is a computational biologist with expertise in cancer immunology and cancer genomics. His work mainly focuses on data integration modeling and machine learning algorithm design for translational cancer research.
